Bio

Neydi Martinez is a bilingual and creative media student currently studying Audio and Video Media Production at Howard Community College. She has over five years of experience supporting children’s growth and development as a nanny, English teacher, and classroom leader. Her strengths include curriculum planning, classroom management, and culturally responsive communication with students of all ages, from elementary school to adult learners. She is an intern on the college’s Marketing & Communications team contributing to visual content and promotional materials. She also plays an active role in student leadership through Ambiciones, a Latino student organization. A proud member of ALPFA (Association of Latino Professionals for America), she is passionate about using media and educational efforts that promote inclusion and social impact. Through her degree in media, she hopes to keep learning how to share stories that matter. Her goal is to create content that helps people feel seen, heard, and connected.
